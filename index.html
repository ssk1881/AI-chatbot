Idea

Sparky is a self learning multipurpose AI chat bot that learns from the user, improves itself and gives better responses every time. Sparky can manage tasks and become your personal assistant to remind you about the events or meetings that you have . If you need someone to talk to sparky can become your bestfriend to listen and react to the things you say in a humane way. Sparky can also be used to respond to customer queries and sparky can answer them and guide them. Link it to your smart home and sparky can takecare of your home and the people inside it. Sparky is bundled with lot of features that would be useful for our everyday life.

Plan
